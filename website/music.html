The 02 Arena


The 02 arena is a multipurpose indoor arena based in North Greenwich it has the second highest indoor seating capacity in the United Kingdom and largest in London. It is now the world busiest music arena. It has direct travel from London tube jubilee line which you access from London Bridge and Stratford station.
